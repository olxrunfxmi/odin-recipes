Slow Cooker Baked Beans Using Canned Beans

Description

These slow cooker baked beans with canned beans are a tasty dish that goes with all your favorite foods and takes just minutes to prepare.

Ingredient

  • Barbecue Sauce
  • Cup Ketchup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Dry Mustard
  • Great Northern Beans
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Large Onion
  • Diced Cooked Ham

Steps

  1. Mix together barbecue sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard in a 4-quart slow cooker until smooth. Stir in beans, bell pepper, onion, and ham until well combined.
  2. Cook on Low until thickened, 8 to 12 hours.
